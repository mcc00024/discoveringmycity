Let's cook!

Work in pairs or small groups to research a festival or tradition from your city

  1. Choose 3 typical dishes from your city and describe them (ingredients, how to cook eat, when people eat it).
  2. Create a digital menu with those dishes. Add drawings, pictures, or decorations.
